Biography

Research with language,
motion, and meaning.

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Mathematics, Hefei University of Technology, advised by Prof. Qingshan Wang. Previously, I worked as an Assistant Algorithm Engineer Intern at iFLYTEK.

My primary research focuses on sign language generation. I am also interested in human pose modeling and video understanding, especially where structured motion and generative learning meet.

01

Sign Language Generation

Translating language representations into fluent and semantically faithful sign motion.

02

Human Pose

Modeling body, hand, and kinematic structure for natural whole-body movement.

03

Video Understanding

Learning temporal and multimodal representations from complex visual sequences.
